Terms of Service

1. PAYMENT TERMS
Because schedules and commission timelines vary, clients may choose one of the following: A. Reserved Slots (1 month or more in advance)
• A deposit is required to secure the slot
• Deposit amount: 20–30% of the total price
• Remaining balance is due once the commission begins B. Immediate or Same-Month Commissions
• Full payment is required upfront
2. REFUND POLICY
• No refunds once the sketching stage has begun
• Chargebacks are not permitted after work has started
3. REVISIONS
A. Sketch Stage
• Unlimited revisions
• Major adjustments are allowed B. Base Coloring Stage
• Major changes are still acceptable
• Color palette changes, expression edits, pose fixes, and clothing adjustments are allowed C. Rendering & Final Stages
• Only minor edits are allowed (lighting, small details, minor corrections)
• Major changes at this stage may require an additional fee
4. USAGE RIGHTS & COPYRIGHT
A. Personal Use (Default)
• Posting, sharing, and printing for personal use is allowed
• Reselling or commercial use is not permitted without a license B. Commercial Use
• Book covers
• Story illustrations
• Merchandise
• Marketing and promotional materials
• Fees vary depending on usage type C. Copyright
• The artist retains full copyright
• Artwork may be displayed in portfolios and social media
• Privacy requests must be made before posting
5. TIMELINE & UPDATES
• Standard turnaround: 2–4 weeks
• Progress updates provided at sketch, base colors, rendering, and final stages
• Delays will always be communicated
6. RUSH REQUESTS
• Rush fees may apply depending on urgency and workload
7. PRIVACY & POSTING
• Finished artwork may be posted with watermark unless requested otherwise
• Posting for book covers may be delayed until official release
8. PAYMENT METHOD
• PayPal only
• Goods & Services available for buyer protection
• Friends & Family optional
• Tips are optional and appreciated
9. ARTIST CREDIT
• Artist credit is required whenever reasonably possible
• Credit may be a signature, caption, or credits section
• Attribution must be clear, readable, and respectful
10. ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE RESTRICTIONS
• Artwork is created without AI tools
• The artwork may not be used for AI training, generation, or alteration
• Uploading to AI platforms is strictly prohibited
• Violations result in immediate termination of usage rights without refund
